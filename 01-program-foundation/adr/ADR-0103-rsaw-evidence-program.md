## Context
ReliabilityFirst audits against Reliability Standard Audit Worksheets (RSAWs). Evidence collected without RSAW alignment risks gaps at audit.

## Decision
Structure the evidence program around **RSAWs per standard** from the outset, mapping every control and artifact to the requirement part it satisfies, stored in a controlled BCSI repository.

## Alternatives Considered
**Collect evidence ad hoc** — rejected: gaps and rework at audit. **Build RSAWs only at audit time** — rejected: rushed and error-prone.

## Consequences
Smooth RF audit; continuous evidence readiness; supports the internal controls program in Phase 08.
