## Context
CIP-003 R1 requires a single senior manager with overall responsibility and authority for CIP compliance, with documented delegations.

## Decision
Designate **Daniel Reyes** (VP Security & Compliance) as **CIP Senior Manager**, with a signed designation and a delegation instrument naming approving delegates for specific CIP actions.

## Alternatives Considered
**Distributed ownership without a single senior manager** — rejected: violates CIP-003 R1. **Undocumented delegation** — rejected: not auditable.

## Consequences
Clear accountability; delegations must be kept current and dated for audit evidence.
