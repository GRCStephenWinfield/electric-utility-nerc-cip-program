## Context
CIP applicability is driven by CIP-002 Attachment 1 impact ratings. GridPoint's control centers and 8 transmission substations meet Medium criteria; generation and remaining substations are Low. No asset meets High criteria.

## Decision
Scope the program to **Medium and Low impact** BES Cyber Systems and their associated EACMS/PACS/PCA; apply Medium-impact requirements to the control centers and 8 substations, and the CIP-003 Attachment 1 subset to Low-impact assets.

## Alternatives Considered
**Assume High** — rejected: not supported by Attachment 1 and adds disproportionate controls. **Low-only** — rejected: understates control-center obligations.

## Consequences
Defines the applicability matrix for every later phase; a future asset change could raise categorization and is tracked via the 15-month CIP-002 review.
