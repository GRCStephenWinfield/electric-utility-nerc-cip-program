## Context
Potential Noncompliance findings must be remediated with evidence before (or transparently disclosed at) the RF audit.

## Decision
Convert all **9 PNCs to formal Mitigation Plans** in Phase 06, each with milestones, owners, and completion evidence.

## Alternatives Considered
**Fix ad hoc** — rejected: no traceability. **Wait for RF to find them** — rejected: risks violations.

## Consequences
Clean Phase 06 handoff; transparent, defensible remediation record.
