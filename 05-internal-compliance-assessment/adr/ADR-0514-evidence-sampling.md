## Context
Testing every artifact is infeasible; RF auditors sample. Sampling must be defensible.

## Decision
Apply **risk-based sampling** across the audit period, weighting higher-risk requirement parts (CIP-005/007/010) with larger samples.

## Alternatives Considered
**Test everything** — rejected: infeasible. **Convenience sampling** — rejected: not defensible.

## Consequences
Efficient, defensible assessment mirroring RF methodology; surfaced 4 new PNCs.
