## Context
A mock audit only rehearses the RF audit well if assessors are independent of the people who implemented the controls.

## Decision
Conduct the internal assessment with the **Advisory Team + Compliance Manager**, independent of implementers (Bell/Nair), examining evidence as an RF auditor would.

## Alternatives Considered
**Self-assessment by implementers** — rejected: not objective. **Skip the mock audit** — rejected: no rehearsal.

## Consequences
Objective findings; realistic RF-audit rehearsal; higher confidence in readiness.
