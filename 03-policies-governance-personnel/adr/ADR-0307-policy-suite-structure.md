## Context
CIP-003-8 R1.1 requires cyber security policies covering nine topics for High/Medium, and R1.2 requires a Low-impact plan per Attachment 1.

## Decision
Maintain **nine discrete policies** (one per required topic) plus a **separate Low-impact security plan**, all approved by the CIP Senior Manager on a 15-month cycle.

## Alternatives Considered
**One monolithic policy** — rejected: hard to map to topics at audit. **Merge Low into Medium policies** — rejected: obscures Attachment 1 scope.

## Consequences
Clean topic-to-policy traceability; closes GAP-18; supports RSAW evidence per topic.
