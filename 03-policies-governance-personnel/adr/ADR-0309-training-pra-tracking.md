## Context
Dispersed training and PRA records (GAP-11, GAP-20) create audit risk and missed renewals.

## Decision
Centralize **training completion and PRA status** in tracking registers with automated due-date reminders (15-month training, 7-year PRA).

## Alternatives Considered
**Spreadsheets per team** — rejected: drift. **Manual tracking** — rejected: missed renewals.

## Consequences
Reliable renewals; closes GAP-11 and GAP-20; single source for CIP-004 evidence.
