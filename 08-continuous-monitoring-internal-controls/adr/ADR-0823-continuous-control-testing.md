## Context
An ICP is only credible if controls are tested and evidenced, not merely documented.

## Decision
Perform **continuous internal control testing** across CIP standards (target ~40 tests/year), self-correcting exceptions and retaining evidence.

## Alternatives Considered
**Annual test only** — rejected: too infrequent. **No testing** — rejected: no assurance.

## Consequences
40 tests/year; controls assessed effective; 2 minor exceptions self-corrected.
