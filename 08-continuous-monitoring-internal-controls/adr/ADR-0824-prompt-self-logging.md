## Context
Minor deviations (e.g., a patch evaluated a few days late) are inevitable; how they are handled affects enforcement posture.

## Decision
**Self-log minimal-risk items promptly** as Compliance Exceptions with remediation, escalating to Self-Report only where warranted.

## Alternatives Considered
**Hide minor items** — rejected: erodes trust and increases penalty risk. **Self-Report everything** — rejected: disproportionate.

## Consequences
Transparent record; 3 items self-logged and remediated; 0 Possible Violations.
