## Context
NERC CIP compliance is continuous and enforced between audits. Without an internal controls program, compliance drifts and issues surface only at the next audit.

## Decision
Stand up a **formal Internal Controls Program (ICP)**: defined control activities, owners, a control-test schedule (~40/yr), and the monitoring calendar, reported to the CIP Senior Manager.

## Alternatives Considered
**Rely on the next audit** — rejected: reactive; risks violations. **Ad hoc monitoring** — rejected: no assurance.

## Consequences
Early detection; sustained good standing; audit-ready evidence at all times.
