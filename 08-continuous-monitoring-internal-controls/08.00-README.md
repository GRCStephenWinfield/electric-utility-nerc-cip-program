## What this phase is

After the favorable ReliabilityFirst Compliance Audit (Phase 07 — 0 new Possible Violations, 1 Area of Concern), GridPoint shifts from *implementation-and-remediation* to **continuous compliance monitoring (ConMon)**. This phase stands up and operates the **Internal Controls Program (ICP)** — the standing engine that keeps the **52 BES Cyber Systems (14 Medium + 38 Low)** and their associated EACMS/PACS/PCA compliant and audit-ready *between* the ~3-year audit cycles, detecting and self-correcting drift before it can mature into a Possible Violation. It also **closes the audit's single Area of Concern** (the CIP-014 Northgate risk assessment, completed 2027-Q4 with independent verification; MIT-05 already closed).
