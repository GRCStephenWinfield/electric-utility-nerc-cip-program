## Context
NERC allows minimal-risk issues to be self-logged (Compliance Exception) while more significant possible violations should be Self-Reported to the Regional Entity.

## Decision
**Self-Report** the 2 Moderate items (MIT-02, MIT-07) to ReliabilityFirst with Mitigation Plans; **self-log** the 7 minimal-risk items.

## Alternatives Considered
**Self-Report everything** — rejected: disproportionate. **Self-log everything** — rejected: understates the Moderate items.

## Consequences
Transparent, proportionate disclosure; enforcement credit for self-reporting.
