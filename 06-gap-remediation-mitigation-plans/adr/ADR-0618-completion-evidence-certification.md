## Context
Mitigation Plan completion must be evidenced and certified to be defensible at audit.

## Decision
Require **Compliance Manager validation** of completion evidence and **CIP Senior Manager certification** for every closed Mitigation Plan.

## Alternatives Considered
**Owner self-closure** — rejected: not independent. **No certification** — rejected: not defensible.

## Consequences
Independently validated closures; audit-ready evidence for 8 closed plans.
