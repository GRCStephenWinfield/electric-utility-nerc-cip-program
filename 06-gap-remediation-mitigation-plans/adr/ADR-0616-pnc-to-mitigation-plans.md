## Context
The 9 Potential Noncompliance findings must be remediated with traceability before the RF audit.

## Decision
Convert all **9 PNCs to formal Mitigation Plans** (MIT-01…09) with milestones, owners, completion dates, and validated evidence.

## Alternatives Considered
**Fix ad hoc** — rejected: no traceability. **Wait for RF** — rejected: risks violations.

## Consequences
Defensible remediation record; clean Phase 07 handoff; 8 of 9 closed pre-audit.
