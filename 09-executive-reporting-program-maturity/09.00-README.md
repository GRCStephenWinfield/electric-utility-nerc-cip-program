## What this phase is

This is the **capstone** of the 9-phase program. It rolls the entire journey up to the **Board and executive leadership**, scores **program maturity** against a 5-level capability model, records the **CIP Senior Manager's annual attestation**, benchmarks GridPoint against industry peers, and sets the **forward 24-month strategic roadmap**. The audience shifts from compliance practitioners and auditors to **directors and executives** — the language is executive, but every claim is still evidence-backed by the prior eight phases.
