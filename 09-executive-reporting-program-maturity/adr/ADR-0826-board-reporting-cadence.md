## Context
NERC CIP compliance is a board-level enterprise risk (penalties up to $1M/violation/day; reliability impact).

## Decision
Establish an **annual CIP briefing to the Board Audit & Risk Committee** by the CIP Senior Manager, with KPIs, risk posture, maturity, and roadmap.

## Alternatives Considered
**No board reporting** — rejected: governance gap. **Ad hoc only** — rejected: inconsistent oversight.

## Consequences
Sustained executive sponsorship; documented oversight; funding endorsed.
