## Context
Maintaining Level 4 requires continued investment; the next RF audit cycle (~2030) is approaching.

## Decision
Commit to a **24-month strategic roadmap** advancing toward Level 5 in the strongest domains (evidence automation, CIP-013, OT monitoring, CIP-012, predictive metrics, IT/OT convergence).

## Alternatives Considered
**Hold at Level 4** — rejected: stagnation risk; emerging standards (CIP-015). **Chase Level 5 everywhere** — rejected: disproportionate cost.

## Consequences
Prioritized, owned initiatives; audit-cycle readiness; regulatory-change resilience.
