## Context
Executives need an objective, repeatable way to understand CIP program strength beyond a binary compliant/non-compliant view.

## Decision
Adopt a **5-level capability maturity model** (Initial→Optimizing) scored per CIP domain, refreshed annually, to communicate program strength and target state.

## Alternatives Considered
**Binary compliant/not** — rejected: no trajectory. **External-only rating** — rejected: costly, less actionable.

## Consequences
Overall Level 4 (Managed) established; per-domain scores drive the roadmap; clear board narrative.
