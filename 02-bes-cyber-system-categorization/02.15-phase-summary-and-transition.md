## Purpose

This document closes **Phase 02 — BES Cyber System Identification & Categorization (CIP-002)** for GridPoint Energy. It confirms that the categorization is baselined and approved, that the pre-implementation gap baseline is established, and that the program is ready to enter **Phase 03 — Policies, Governance & Personnel**.

## 1. What Phase 02 Delivered

| Outcome | Result |
|---|---|
| BES asset inventory reconciled | 2 Control Centers · 44 substations (8 Medium / 34 Low / 2 out of scope) · 4 generation plants |
| BES Cyber Systems categorized | **52 BCS = 14 Medium + 38 Low** (0 High) |
| Associated systems identified | EACMS 26 · PACS 18 · PCA 60; ~420 BCAs |
| Attachment 1 criteria applied | Criterion 2.12 (control centers), 2.5 (345 kV substations); default Low elsewhere |
| CIP-002 categorization approved | Signed by CIP Senior Manager **Daniel Reyes**, 2026-03-02 (02.09) |
| Applicability scoped | **118 applicable requirement parts** (02.10) |
| Baseline gap assessment | **84 met (71%) / 34 gaps (29%)** (02.11) |
| Gap register risk-ranked | 34 gaps: **6 High · 15 Moderate · 13 Low** (02.12) |
| Remediation roadmap | High-first sequencing across Phases 03–06 (02.13) |
| Review schedule set | CIP-002 R2 15-month cycle + interim triggers (02.14) |

## 2. Baseline Established

Two baselines are now frozen and govern all subsequent phases:

1. **Categorization baseline** — the approved 14 Medium / 38 Low / 0 High BES Cyber System list (02.09), subject to 15-month review (02.14).
2. **Compliance baseline** — 84 of 118 applicable parts met; 34 gaps risk-ranked and time-phased (02.12, 02.13).

## 6. Open Items Carried Into Phase 03

- The 34 gaps (02.12) become the remediation backlog; High gaps GAP-01…06 begin immediately with interim mitigations active.
- Sunfield Solar and control-center modernization remain flagged for interim recategorization (02.14).
- CIP-014 final applicability for the candidate critical station is confirmed via a later risk assessment (referenced in 02.10).

## 7. Handoff to Phase 03

Phase 03 will establish the CIP cyber security policies (CIP-003 R1), the governance and personnel controls (CIP-004: training, PRA, access management/revocation), and the BCSI protection program (CIP-011), directly consuming this phase's approved categorization, applicability matrix, and gap register. The phase inherits the frozen categorization and compliance baselines without modification; any change to the categorization must route through the CIP-002 R2 review process (02.14).
